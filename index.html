What is Vehicle Hire Network?

Vehicle Hire Network is a connected community of over 400 independent rental companies across England, Northern Ireland, Scotland and Wales.

With a collective fleet of over 75,000 vehicles including cars, vans, trucks, minibuses and campers, we’re sure to have the vehicle you need.

Whether you are moving home, delivering chilled food, going on holiday or need a company car, our unique search facility will find the vehicle you need in seconds.

Support your local rental company

Vehicle Hire Network is made up of over 650 rental locations across the UK so wherever you are, we’ll have a local operator to assist with your needs.

Members are also likely to be family run businesses that pride themselves on delivering high levels of customer care. That’s why we love independent rental.

Find the vehicle you need in seconds

Our unique search platform only displays vehicles that are actually available for your chosen dates, reducing search time and getting you on the road quicker.

Our unique platform will search over 75,000 vehicles in just seconds and display vehicles and rental operators near you.

Need a specific model?

Use our model search facility to find a specific vehicle and view its location on a map. We’ll only display the vehicle if the rental company actually has one available, even when completing a model search.
